Tekken 7 Fated Retribution Rage Attack Trailer

Tekken 7 Fated Retribution introduces a new gameplay mechanic called Rage Attack. This mechanic can be seen as the Tekken side of character's version of EX attacks that give new offensive options for each character. To help get acquainted with this mechanic, Bandai Namco have just uploaded an introduction trailer to this mechanic that goes over the cast's new moves. Check it out below!
